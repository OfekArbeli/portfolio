let me tell you about myself...

A Designer

My name is Ofek Daniel Arbeli, I'm 29 years old from Herzeliya, Israel.

My journey starts right after I came back from a big trip around the world, I came with a big insight - if I could work in something that I love, I wouldn't 'work' even a single day in my life.

So back then, I knew I love to create, so I went to art school and started a degree in 'visual communications', where I learned UX and UI.

After 2 years, I realized that as a designer, I wasn't able to create my ideas as I was visualizing them in my mind. I was missing the coding part.

A Developer

For that I went to a fullstack Bootcamp course for 8 months, to learn how to be a front-end developer.

That was the best decision I have ever made. In the course, I flourished, I was able to combine my UX skills with the front-end skills and create beautiful componenets.

Becuase of that, I started to take projects as a freelancer, when my main target was to sharpen my skills and to study as much as possible.

After the course, I felt like I was finally able to create anything which comes to my mind and I started to work as a full-stack developer in Amdocs.

As a full-stack developer, I learned a lot of technologies, but my main interest was and still is, to be a front end ux developer.

A Person

On My free time, I love hanging on with my friends, watch movies, draw and paint. I'm a learner, I found everything intresting so I have the weirdest google search history.
